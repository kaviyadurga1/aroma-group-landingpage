FAQS

Need answers?

Got questions? We've got answers. Learn more about Aroma Group and how we can transform your business.

01 What industries does your Aroma Group companies operate in?

Our group operates across multiple sectors, including construction, hospitality (hotels, cafés, and restaurants), film production, and financial services such as investment and fund management. This diversified structure allows us to deliver value across different markets.

02 Are all the companies under one management?

Yes. While each company operates independently with its own specialized team, they function under a unified strategic vision and governance framework set by the group's leadership.

03 Can I engage with more than one company under the group?

Absolutely. Clients, partners, and investors can work with one or multiple companies within the group, depending on their requirements. Each business unit is equipped to handle sector-specific needs.

04 How can I contact a specific company within the group?

You can visit the individual company pages on our website for direct contact details, or reach out through the main contact form, and our team will guide your inquiry to the appropriate company.

05 Does the Aroma Group accept partnerships or investment opportunities?

Yes. We welcome strategic partnerships and investment discussions across our business verticals. Please contact us through the official inquiry form for further details.

06 Where are your companies located and do you operate internationally?

Our group has operations in multiple locations and serves both local and international markets. Specific operational regions vary by company and are detailed on their respective pages.
